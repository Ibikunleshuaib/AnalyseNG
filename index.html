How it works

Step 1: Tell us about your research/data

Write brief description of what your research is about in the form below or click on the whatsapp icon on the left bottom to chat with a data analyst.

Step 2: Forward your data

Attach softcopy of your research data in the form below or send via whatsapp. If your data is in hard copy form (e.g questionnaires), state it in the message box in the form below or click on the whatsapp icon to be linked to our logistics agent nearest to you who will collect the data from you and deliver it to our versatile data analysts.

Step 3: Get results

Within a week or two of receipt of your data, our team of highly skilled data analysts would forward to you results of analysis carried out including detailed interpretation.

AnalyseNG

AnalyseNG is a full-fledged research and data analytics service provider. We help students, businesses and organizations define methodology of research projects, analyse research data using appropriate statistical tools and interpret results of data analysis in detailed and explanatory form. Our suite of services include data analysis, design and analysis of questionnaire, design and analysis of experiment, market research, business intelligence, academic research service, training and consultancy services.
